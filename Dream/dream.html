Dream House

            One of my dreams is to live in a large, comfortable, high-tech, and elegant home. My ideal home is something that will reflect my personality. For me, a house is quite significant in our lives because we spend the most of our time there, and it must make you feel at ease. I'd like my home to be basic yet elegant at the same time, so I'll probably go for a minimalist design with a lot of glass to save energy. There will be a swimming pool so that if it gets too hot outside due to the sun, I can cool down in the pool. The house will be surrounded by a huge garden with numerous trees and flowers. The flowers would provide a tropical atmosphere in the home. There would be a lot of free space in the backyard that could be utilized for a party or an extra garage because I am also a fan of cars. I chose white to paint all of the walls in the house's inside since it is clean and complements the minimalist style. On the first floor, I plan to include a movie room, two play rooms, and a guest living area in the middle. The kitchen would be contemporary and would be located behind the dining room. I'd have three bedrooms and one master bedroom on the second story. My preferred style for my dream house would be painted white on all of the walls inside the house since it is clean and complements the minimalist style.
